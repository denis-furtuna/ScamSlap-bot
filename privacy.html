Privacy Policy for ScamSlap

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

ScamSlap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our Messenger bot to detect AI-generated media (Deepfakes).

2. Information We Collect

We do not store, share, or sell your personal data. When you interact with ScamSlap, we temporarily process:

  • Media files (Images/Videos): Forwarded securely to our scanning partner (Sightengine) strictly for AI analysis. They are not saved on our servers.
  • Facebook Sender ID & Locale: Used exclusively to reply to your messages in your native language.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the Sightengine API to analyze media. We do not send them any personally identifiable user information, only the raw media links.

4. Data Retention

Our backend server acts strictly as a pass-through. Media and messages are processed in memory and immediately discarded after the verdict is returned to Facebook Messenger. We have no databases and keep no logs of user conversations.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official ScamSlap Facebook Page.
